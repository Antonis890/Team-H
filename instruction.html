What is Treasure Hunt?

The treasure hunt is an online quiz game where a player earns point by answering different questions. To find information for answers, you need to go specific locations in real life.

How to play?

  • Click on the "Let's get started!" button on the main page
  • Type the desired player name and click on "Load Available Hints"
  • Allow game to access your geolocation
  • Answer the question and go to a location
  • Each correctly answered question will give you score points
  • After finishing with questions, you can check the leaderboard
